Large-Format Displays

Abena Display Stand

71” x 28” x 16” display stand

This display stand was built to hold different product combinations, depending on the retailer’s or event’s needs. During the design process, I worked with our vendor to create a structure that could be viewed from all angles (even the back), to allow flexibility of placement within stores. For a seamless setup process, I digitally illustrated instructions that accompanied each stand.
