Work About

I’m a Florida-raised, Los Angeles-based graphic designer and marketing manager who currently works in-house for a sustainable international healthcare brand, Abena North America, Inc.  Designing in-house has allowed me to delve into every step of the branding process, from the granular projects of packaging, product renderings, web assets, printed marketing material, and UX & UI design to curating trade show booth layouts, display concepts, e-commerce campaigns, and managing a team of marketeers.  I’m passionate about presenting ideas in a clear and logical way while also exuding the brand’s mission through aesthetics.  When not in the office, you can find me backpacking a nearby mountain range, painting at home, attending electro-funk concerts, or exploring new restaurants.  I look forward to connecting and collaborating with other creative and eco-minded individuals - feel free to give me a shout!
