6” x 4” cards

Coupon cards created for sampling programs and events. They were designed to act like an “elevator pitch” for our products, as this card was sometimes a customer’s first interaction with the brand. Bambo Nature Baby Gift Box

12” x 11” x 7” retail box for diapers, wipes & skincare

This gift box was crafted with new moms in mind. It holds a full range of baby products (diapers, wipes & skincare) and a Bambo Nature Booklet, to be ideal for baby showers and as an introductory set. To mimic the appearance of wrapping paper, I arranged the designed exterior print so that the pattern is seamless on all edges. The interior is designed so that the parent experiences multiple “reveals”. Currently sold online at BamboNatureUSA.com, Amazon, Walmart, and Nordstrom.

Large-Format Displays

Bambo Nature Trade Show Booth

20’ x 10‘ trade show booth

This trade show booth was created to use at trade show events, visited by thousands of attendees. I was given the task of curating a space in which my team could talk with new business partners, entertain children, and display products. With the idea of creating a cozy, home-like space, I created an illusion of two separate spaces in which our products are used - a nursery and bathroom. Various props accompanied the backdrop to allow for storage and flow of foot traffic.

Bambo Nature Display Unit

55” x 19” x 12” product display unit

This display unit was built to hold different product combinations, depending on the retailer’s or event’s needs. During the design process, I worked one-on-one with our structural vendor, created multiple small-scale mock ups, and got feedback from retailers, all while ensuring the product’s branding and features were displayed clearly.

Bambo Nature Store Sign at Organic Basic Foods

56” x 103” window vinyl

As part of a retail partnership with Organic Basic Foods in Hoboken, NJ, I created this store sign to showcase the Bambo Nature products they carry. When designing this piece, I took the pedestrians’ POV into consideration and made sure that the most important information was at eye-level and quick to read.
